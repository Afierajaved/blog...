Our Departments

Department of Bchlors of Nursing

The BSN program is designed to prepare professional nurses with advanced knowledge and clinical skills in patient care, disease prevention, and healthcare management. With a strong foundation in medical sciences, leadership, and evidence-based practice, graduates are equipped to work in hospitals, clinics, and community healthcare settings, providing high-quality nursing care and promoting overall wellness.

Department of Bachlors of Midwifery

The BSM program is designed to train skilled midwives in maternal and neonatal care, emphasizing safe childbirth, prenatal and postnatal care, and reproductive health. With a strong focus on evidence-based practices and hands-on clinical training, graduates are prepared to provide compassionate and high-quality care to mothers and newborns in hospitals, clinics, and community settings.

Department of Certified Nursing Assistant

The CNA department provides foundational training in patient care, assisting nurses, and supporting healthcare teams in hospitals and clinics. Our program emphasizes hands-on skills, compassionate care, and essential medical procedures to prepare students for a rewarding career in nursing.

Department of Lady Health Visitor

The LHV department focuses on maternal and child healthcare, community health education, and preventive care. Our program equips students with the skills to provide essential healthcare services, promote wellness, and support women and children in both urban and rural communities.
