ABOUT SCNMAS

Nursing is a profession within the Health Care Sector focused on the care of individuals, families, and communities so they may attain maintain or recover optimal health and quality of life. Nursing care for individuals of all ages and all cultural backgrounds who are healthy and ill in a holistic manner based on the individuals physical, emotional, psychological, intellectual, social, and spiritual needs.
The profession combines physical sciences, social sciences and technology in caring for those individuals -in order to provide quality nursing care there is need to train nurses effectively to work as team member of health care delivery system. It is therefore their training should involve positive attitude, knowledge, skills, professional expertise, latest trends in health care needed of the community in the country as a whole.
SCN is dedicated to excellence in nursing teaching, scholarships, practice and professional service. Nursing education is a unique practice discipline with its own national accrediting standards in legislated licensure requirements.

MISSION

The mission of SCN is to help youth in the acquisition of academic and moral excellence, through providing best possible facilities, training educators, enhancing technical skills, and providing high-quality education and training to prepare competent nursing professionals, with a commitment to patient care, community service, and health promotion.

VISION

To establish a knowledge-based, skillful, research and career-oriented students that may struggle for the advancement of the nursing profession and contribute towards a healthy state and society. Aspiring to be a leader in nursing education, fostering innovation, research, and excellence in healthcare.

Goals

Our nursing college is dedicated to providing education that equips students with clinical knowledge, critical thinking, and technical skills for effective patient care. We foster empathy, professionalism, and ethical responsibility to prepare nurses to advocate for patients with dignity. Emphasizing lifelong learning, we ensure graduates stay updated with evolving healthcare practices and research. Additionally, we develop leadership and teamwork skills, empowering nurses to collaborate within multidisciplinary teams and improve patient outcomes.

Philosophy

Our institute is committed to developing individuals into complete professionals who positively impact the nursing profession and their communities. Through academic, artistic, and innovative pursuits, students grow into lifelong learners and ethical leaders. We empower future nurses with the knowledge, skills, and compassion needed for holistic, evidence-based care. By fostering leadership and lifelong learning, we prepare them to excel in diverse healthcare environments. Dedicated to patient-centered care, our graduates advocate for well-being and collaborate effectively in global healthcare settings.
